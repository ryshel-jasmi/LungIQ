LungIQ is an AI-powered diagnostic tool designed to assist in early detection of lung-related conditions such as COVID-19 and pneumonia, using chest X-rays.
LungIQ analyzes chest X-ray images using machine learning models to detect signs of lung diseases such as COVID-19, pneumonia, and other conditions.
